Rules:

It's easy! You'll be given a movie title. Name an actor in that movie. Then name another movie that actor was in. And on and on! You will get strikes for incorrect guesses (check your spelling!). Three strikes and yer out!

You're allowed to name a movie or an actor more than once. However, you can only make a connection between a particular actor and a particular movie once.

For example:

The Basketball Diaries > Leonardo Dicaprio > The Departed > Mark Wahlberg > The Basketball Diaries
is allowed.

But

The Basketball Diaries > Leonardo Dicaprio > The Basketball Diaries
is not.
